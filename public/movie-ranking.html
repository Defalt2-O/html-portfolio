The Best Animes According to Rithin.

My top 3 animes of all-time.

Naruto

Best Anime ever. Has an amazing storyline and I personally relate to the main character.

Attack On Titan

Incredible storyline and visuals. The unexpected twists and turns are in a realm of its own.

Kengan Ashura

An anime about the underground gangs that hunger for power, and wrestle for its control by using representative fighters to obtain it.
